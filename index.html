Dante
Interstellar

Dante Interstellar is a Berlin-based producer and engineer working across electronic music, trap, and hip-hop. His sound navigates the space between club pressure and cosmic atmosphere — textured, deliberate, and built to move.

Drawing from Berlin's dense underground and a restless appetite for genre collisions, Dante crafts tracks that resist easy categories. Whether sculpting bass-heavy trap architecture or threading melodic tension through electronic frameworks, the work always carries a singular weight.

In 2024 he delivered a remix for Raël's "No Hard Feelings" EP on Diffuse Reality Records — a record that drew attention across the electronic community and placed him in the company of artists pushing the edges of contemporary club music.

With releases on Beatport and a growing presence across streaming platforms, Dante Interstellar is building a body of work rooted in craft, risk, and uncompromising sound design.
